Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

The Mr. Lube Training App (“the App”) is used internally by participating Mr. Lube franchise locations to support employee onboarding, training progress, and supervisor reviews. This Privacy Policy explains what information the App collects and how it is used.

Information We Collect

The App collects only the information necessary for employee training and internal operations, including:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • User ID (automatically generated)
  • Training progress updates
  • Notes submitted by supervisors or trainees

How Information Is Used

The information collected is used only for training, onboarding, communication between staff, and tracking employee progress. No information is sold, shared, or used for marketing.

Data Storage

All data is stored securely in Firebase and is accessible only to approved managers, supervisors, and administrators for internal operations.

Third-Party Services

The App uses Firebase Authentication and Firestore to safely manage login, user accounts, and training data. These services store user information only for internal app functionality.

Children’s Privacy

This App is not intended for public use or for individuals under 13. Access is restricted to employees invited by participating Mr. Lube franchise locations.

Data Access & Removal

Employees may request access to or removal of their information by contacting their store administrator or management team.

Contact

For questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the App administrator at: mrlubetrainingapp@gmail.com
